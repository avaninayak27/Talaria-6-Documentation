Introduction

The Talaria-6 family of modules features an industry-leading digital radio architecture that significantly extends the battery life of IoT devices.

Key Features: * Low Power: Up to 50% less power consumption compared to competitors. * Integrated Stack: Full Wi-Fi and Bluetooth LE stack. * Small Footprint: Ideal for wearables and smart home sensors.
