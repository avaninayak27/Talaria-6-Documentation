Green Tea Studio
================

Setup Instructions
------------------

The Talaria 6 family is a suite of comprehensive SoCs featuring ultra-low-power Wi-Fi 7, multi-protocol connectivity, and enhanced cybersecurity. By integrating the high-performance Green Tea Studio™ (GTS) development environment, the Talaria 6 platform provides a trusted, secure, and fast path to commercial-grade IoT solutions.

This next-generation platform empowers developers to upgrade to the award-winning Talaria 6 hardware while preserving existing software investments. By plugging directly into open-source frameworks, Green Tea Studio eliminates development friction—accelerating time to market while delivering:

* Higher Throughput, Lower Power, and Better Coverage
* Increased Compute Power and Security
* Solutions for Rapid Time to Market

Introduction
------------
The Talaria-6 family of modules features an industry-leading digital radio architecture that significantly extends the battery life of IoT devices.
